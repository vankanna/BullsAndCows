Description: Cows and Bulls is a guessing game where you use feedback from the cows and bulls to help guess the magic number. Rules: Guess must be 4 unique digits 0-9

If you see a Cow, it means you have the right number in the wrong location.

If you see a Bull it means you have the right number in the right location.

Use that logic to get 4 bulls and match the number.
